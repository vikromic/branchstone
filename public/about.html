ABOUT ME

I'm Viktoriia, the artist behind Branchstone, a mixed-media project inspired by natural textures and quiet details. I work with found materials like wood, moss, and resin to create abstract compositions that preserve the raw beauty of nature.

Originally from Ukraine and now based in Texas, my work is a personal reflection on change, resilience, and the grounding power of the natural world.

ARTIST STATEMENT

My work explores the emotional connection between natural materials and personal memory. Each piece begins with an organic element — wood, moss, bark, algae — and evolves into an abstract composition.

I aim to create work that invites quiet reflection, offering a sense of grounding and connection to the natural world. Most of my pieces begin with a small moment—something I found on a walk, something I couldn't leave behind.
